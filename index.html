Welcome to Shree Sant Acchyut Maharaj Heart Hospital And research Amravati Hospital

Shri Sant Acchyut Maharaj Heart Hospital in Amravati is a dedicated cardiac care center on Mardi Road, known for advanced heart treatments, surgeries (like angioplasty/bypass), modern tech, skilled cardiologists (e.g., Dr. Shailesh Jayade, Dr. Sudhir Dhande), and a serene environment, providing affordable care under schemes like Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana, making it a vital resource for the region's heart patients.

About

Shri Sant Achyut Maharaj Heart Hospital (SSAMHH) is a tertiary care cardiac centre located on Mardi Road, Amravati, Maharashtra. Established to honor the vision of Shri Sant Achyut Maharaj, our hospital provides comprehensive cardiac care, including diagnostics, interventional procedures, and cardiac surgery. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of doctors and support staff, SSAMHH aims to deliver high-quality, affordable cardiac treatment under national healthcare schemes such as MJPJAY and PMJAY. श्री संत अच्युत महाराज हार्ट हॉस्पिटल (SSAMHH) हे मार्डी रोड, अमरावती येथे स्थित tertiary care हृदयरोग रुग्णालय आहे. श्री संत अच्युत महाराज यांच्या सेवाभावना आणि दृष्टिकोनातून या रुग्णालयाची स्थापना करण्यात आली आहे. येथे हृदयरोगाचे निदान, अँजिओग्राफी, अँजिओप्लास्टी, तसेच हृदयशस्त्रक्रिया अशा सर्व सेवा उपलब्ध आहेत. आधुनिक सुविधा आणि अनुभवी डॉक्टरांच्या टीमसह SSAMHH मध्ये MJPJAY आणि PMJAY योजनेअंतर्गत दर्जेदार आणि परवडणाऱ्या दरात उपचार दिले जातात.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide compassionate, patient-centric cardiac care to all sections of society, especially underprivileged patients, by leveraging state-of-the-art technology and skilled medical professionals, adhering to highest standards. Vision: To be recognized as the leading heart hospital in the India, committed to excellence, innovation, and community welfare. ध्येय (Mission): State-of-the-art तंत्रज्ञान आणि कुशल वैद्यकीय तज्ज्ञांच्या माध्यमातून, जागतिक दर्जाच्या (world-class) सेवा देत, प्रत्येक रुग्णाला – विशेषतः गरजू व वंचित घटकांतील रुग्णांना – सहानुभूतीपूर्वक आणि रुग्णकेंद्रित हृदयरोग उपचार सेवा पुरवणे. दृष्टी (Vision): उत्कृष्टता, नवोपक्रम आणि समाजकल्याणासाठी वचनबद्ध असलेले, भारतातील अग्रगण्य हृदयरोग रुग्णालय म्हणून ओळख निर्माण करणे.

Our Services

  • Angiography:-

    Advanced diagnostic imaging to assess coronary artery diseases.

  • Angioplasty & Stenting:-

    Primary and elective procedures with latest stent technologies.

  • Cardiac Surgery:-

    Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG), valve replacements, Minimally Invasive Cardiac Surgery (MICS).

  • Echocardiography & Doppler:-

    Including Transesophageal echocardiography (TEE) and stress echocardiography

  • ICU & CCU Care:-

    24×7 critical care units with trained intensivists and modern multipara monitors.

  • Pacemaker & Device Implantation:-

    Single-chamber, dual-chamber pacemakers, AICD, and CRT devices.
